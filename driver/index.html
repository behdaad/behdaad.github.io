Snapp! Driver app is the counterpart to the Snapp! Passenger app. Drivers use this app to view ride requests and decide whether to accept one or not. Some of its most important features include:

  • View ride requests and their data
  • Update ride status
  • Chat with Passenger
  • View Passenger’s payment status
  • Update vehicle registration info

Nearly a year after the successful launch of the rewritten Passenger app we decided to do the same with the Driver app: rewrite the legacy Objective-C code in Swift using SNPArchitecture.
